Mariana Pasta

Description


This Delicous Roasted chicken recipie is easy, fast and feeds alot of people. 10min prep and 40min realx perfect solution for lazy dinner and surprise guests.


Ingredients

  • Pasta Penne
  • Mariana Sauce
  • Thyme, Ginger

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta, save the pasta water
  2. Pour pasta water and mariana sauce in a pan
  3. Add some Thyme and Ginger
  4. Add Pasta and cook for a minute
  5. Your Mariana Sauce Pasta is ready
