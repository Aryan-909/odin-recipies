Stir Fry Noodles

Description


This Delicous Roasted chicken recipie is easy, fast and feeds alot of people. 10min prep and 40min realx perfect solution for lazy dinner and surprise guests.


Ingredients

  • Dry Rice noodels
  • Chicken Breasts (1pc)
  • Broccoli 200gms
  • Soy sauce, oil, salt

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into short strips and cook in a pan
  2. Do the same for Broccoli
  3. put some soy sauce and let it cook
  4. Hydrate the noodles and fry them in the same pan
  5. Your Stir Fry Noodles are ready!!
