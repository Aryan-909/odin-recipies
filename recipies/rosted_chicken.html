Roasted Chicken

Description


This Delicous Roasted chicken recipie is easy, fast and feeds alot of people. 10min prep and 40min realx perfect solution for lazy dinner and surprise guests.


Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Oregano
  • Red Chilli Flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix Oilve oil, oregano and Chilli sauce
  2. Coat the Chicken with this mixture
  3. Roast the chicken in the oven for 50min at 375°F
  4. Your Roasted Chicken is ready
